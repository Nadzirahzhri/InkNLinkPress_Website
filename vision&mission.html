Our Vision and Mission

Our Vision

Through the self-publishing platform offered by Link and Ink Press, authors can market their books both digitally and physically. By providing a platform, our company ensures to be a bridge between authors and readers. Linking internal connections between literature works and literary communities and making sure the reading culture continues to thrive in the future.

Our Mission

The mission of Link and Ink Press is to publish high-quality writing that expands perspective and improves lives while using storytelling to create. By providing space for authors and creative minds to develop their work, our company is dedicated to making sure that all reading materials are available to readers all around the world.
