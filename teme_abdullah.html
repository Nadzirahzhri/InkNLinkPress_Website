TEME ABDULLAH
AUTHOR | ARCHITECT

Who is Teme Abdullah ?

Known for his involvement in the fields of architecture, writing, and painting, Teme first emerged on the internet around 2008 as a blogger focusing on comic and doodle-themed content, infused with elements of Islamic teachings. This was a hobby he pursued as a high school student.

Born and raised in Selangor, Teme completed his secondary education at a fully residential school (SBP) also located in Selangor. After completing the Malaysian Certificate of Education (SPM), he continued his higher education, earning a Bachelorâ€™s degree in Architecture in the United Kingdom. Upon graduating, Teme worked at an architectural firm in London.

Teme uses a pseudonym given to him by his friends during his secondary school years, which he continues to use as his pen name to this day. The name "Abdullah" was added by the author himself at the end of "Teme" when he first began writing books in 2016. The pronunciation of "Teme" is similar to how the word teman (meaning "friend") is pronounced in the Kelantanese dialect. The word teman can also serve as a first-person pronoun, meaning "I" or "me." In short, the combination of Teme and Abdullah translates to "I am merely a servant of Allah."
