About Ink N Link Press

Ink N Link Press was founded with a mission to support small, passionate authors who face barriers in getting their stories published. We offer a range of services from printing to digital publishing, ensuring that each authorâ€™s work is presented professionally. Our business aims to make publishing more accessible for writers, offering them the tools and guidance they need to turn their dreams into reality.

Our team consists of individuals who share a love for literature and publishing. We are united by the desire to provide aspiring authors with the resources and guidance they need to succeed. As a partnership, we work closely together, making important decisions that align with our shared goals, ensuring the best possible outcomes for our clients and our company.

Based in Shah Alam, Selangor, we are strategically positioned to reach a wide audience, including authors, retailers, and book lovers across the country. Our location allows us to stay connected with key players in the literature and publishing industries, fostering new opportunities for growth.

Our Journey

Since our inception, Ink N Link Press has been dedicated to making an impact in the publishing world. Here's a glimpse of our journey:

  • May 2023

    Ink N Link Press was officially registered as a partnership business.

  • July 2023

    Launched our services, offering beginner authors a platform to publish their works.

  • October 2023

    Participated in the National Book Fair, showcasing our first 20 publications.

  • January 2024

    Hosted a successful series of workshops on creative writing, helping local authors hone their craft.

  • April 2024

    Expanded our services to include digital publishing, offering eBooks and audiobooks.

  • July 2024

    Celebrated the publication of 100 books and launched a new series of author mentoring programs.

Looking Ahead

As we continue to grow, Ink N Link Press is committed to becoming the leading self-publishing platform for aspiring authors. Our goal is to provide more tools and resources for authors, expand our digital publishing services, and increase our impact across the literary community. We envision a future where more stories are shared with the world, and every passionate writer has a chance to be heard.

Join us on our journey as we work to make dreams come true, one page at a time.
