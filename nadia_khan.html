NADIA KHAN
AUTHOR | SCREENWRITER | ACTOR

Who is Nadia Khan?

Nadia Mohamad Hatta, also known as Nadia Khan, earned her M.B.B.S. in 2009 and currently balances roles as a part-time tutor, a full-time writer, and a dedicated procrastinator. She's also dabbling in philosophy and filmmaking, though these pursuits are still works in progress. Her writing journey took off when Buku Fixi discovered her talent after she won a national scriptwriting competition.

Her debut novel, *Kelabu*, was published in 2011, followed by a short story collection titled *Cerpen* in 2012, which included her contribution to another anthology, *Kopi* (2012). Her first script adaptation, *Aku, Dia dan Tong Sampah*, was turned into a telemovie by Playground Productions.

Currently, she is teaching at a medical faculty in a Malaysian university while pursuing a Master's degree in Psychology. Now, her most famous book was "Children We Never Had".
