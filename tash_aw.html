TASH AW

Who is Tash Aw ?

Tash Aw or Aw Ta-shi born in Taiwan to Malaysian parents, Tash Aw spent his childhood in Kuala Lumpur before moving to England as a teenager. He studied law at the University of Cambridge and the University of Warwick, eventually relocating to London to pursue writing. While working various jobs, including a four-year stint as a lawyer, he completed his debut novel during a creative writing program at the University of East Anglia.

Aw’s work has garnered significant acclaim, winning the Whitbread and Commonwealth Prizes, an O. Henry Award, and earning two longlist nominations for the MAN Booker Prize. His fiction has been translated into 23 languages, establishing him as the most commercially successful Malaysian author in recent years.

Following his literary success, he became a prominent figure in Malaysia and Singapore and is now recognized as one of Southeast Asia’s most respected literary voices.
