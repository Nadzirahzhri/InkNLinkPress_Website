Our Services
Manuscript Evaluation
After we receive the work, we analyse it carefully and provide any changes that are required based on the content, for example, initial reading and improvement recommendations.
Editing and Proofreading
We will examine its content to ensure there is no error in it. The content is from language, word count, and fiction or non-fiction manuscripts.
Design
Our company will consider our client's preferences regarding colours, typography, and additional objects or elements in this section.
Printing
Our company offers several kinds of options in the form of books for printing services. Make in factors like the book's size, the printing style, the cover page, the size of the paper, the colours, the binding, and laminating process.
Distribution
We additionally offer our clients a book distribution service. The clients can choose online or physical distribution.
Marketing and Promotion
Our company also offers marketing and promotion services, such as promoting our clients' books through social media, and book launch events.
E-book Conversion
Our company also provides e-book conversion services.
ISBN and Copyright Registration
This service is offered to clients because it can help safeguard and manage literary works in the marketplace, providing recognition and protection for authors.
Consultation Service
For our clients, we are also providing consultation services. The service is subject-based and charged at an hourly rate.
